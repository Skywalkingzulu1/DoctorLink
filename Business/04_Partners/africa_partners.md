## Partnership Strategy

### Priority Partners (Immediate)
1. Health ministries for regulatory support
2. Medical councils for doctor licensing
3. Major hospitals for referral network

### Secondary Partners (Growth)
1. Insurance companies for coverage
2. NGOs for distribution
3. Telecom for reach

### Strategic Partners (Scale)
1. Pharma for medication access
2. Development finance for funding
3. Tech platforms for integration
