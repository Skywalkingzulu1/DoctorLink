# DoctorLink - Executive Summary

## The Company

**DoctorLink** is Africa's first gig economy telemedicine platform - connecting patients with doctors through a flexible, on-demand model where doctors work as independent gig professionals.

## The Problem

Africa faces a healthcare crisis:
- **1.3 doctors per 10,000 people** (vs. 36 in Europe)
- **60% of population** lives in rural areas with minimal healthcare
- **500M+ people** live in healthcare deserts
- **Waiting times** of weeks to months for appointments
- **Healthcare costs** push 11M Africans into poverty annually

## The Solution

DoctorLink provides on-demand video consultations with licensed doctors through a gig economy model:

1. **Patients** - Book appointments instantly, choose service tiers, pay transparent prices
2. **Doctors** - Set own prices, work when available, keep 80% of fees
3. **Platform** - 20% platform fee enables sustainability

### Key Features
- Video consultations (WebRTC)
- Service tiers: Quick Chat, Video Call, Full Consultation, Prescription Review, Report Analysis
- Dynamic pricing (doctor-set + peak pricing)
- Tipping (100% to doctor)
- Inconvenience discounts (10% for issues)
- Gig mode (doctors go online/offline)
- Hashgraph blockchain integration (future)

- Platform revenue: $3 (20%)
