## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Key Regulatory Considerations

#### South Africa
- **HPCSA**: Health Professions Council regulates telemedicine
- **POPIA**: Data protection similar to GDPR
- **Telemedicine Guidelines**: Emerging frameworks

#### Nigeria
- **MDCN**: Medical and Dental Council oversight
- **NDPR**: National Data Protection Regulation
- **Telemedicine Guidelines**: Being developed

#### Kenya
- **KMPDB**: Kenya Medical Practitioners Board
- **Data Protection**: GDPR-aligned
- **mHealth Regulations**: Ministry developing

### Regulatory Opportunities

1. **Sandbox Programs**: Regulatory sandboxes in several countries
2. **Government Support**: Digital health strategies supportive
3. **Regional Harmonization**: African Union frameworks emerging

## 8. Opportunity Assessment

### Strengths
1. **Unique Model**: First gig economy telemedicine in Africa
2. **Technology**: Blockchain/Hedera integration planned
3. **Market Timing**: Africa digital health growing rapidly
