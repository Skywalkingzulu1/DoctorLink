# Revenue Projections - Financial Feasibility Study

This document provides detailed 5-year revenue projections, unit economics, and financial modeling for DoctorLink.

---

## 1. Revenue Model Overview

### Revenue Streams

| Stream | Description | 20% Platform Fee |
|--------|-------------|------------------|
| **Consultation Revenue** | Primary revenue from consultations | 20% to platform |
| **Tip Revenue** | Optional tips (100% to doctor, not platform) | N/A |
| **Discount Revenue** | Inconvenience discounts reduce revenue | From patient balance |
| **Enterprise** | B2B contracts (future) | Negotiated |

### Revenue Math
- Patient pays: $15 (example)
- Platform fee (20%): $3
- Doctor receives (80%): $12
- Tips: Separate (100% to doctor)

## 4. Unit Economics

### Per Consultation Economics

| Metric | Value | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **Patient Price** | $13-15 | Tiered pricing |
| **Platform Fee (20%)** | $2.60-3.00 | Doctor keeps $10.40-12 |
| **Platform Cost** | $1.50-2.00 | Infrastructure, support |
| **Contribution Margin** | $1.00-1.50 | 35-40% margin |

### Customer Economics

| Metric | Conservative | Aggressive |
|--------|--------------|------------|
| **CAC (Patient)** | $8-12 | $5-8 |
| **CAC (Doctor)** | $10-15 | $8-10 |
| **LTV (Patient)** | $80-120 | $150-200 |
| **LTV (Doctor)** | $200-400 | $400-800 |
| **LTV:CAC (Patient)** | 8-10x | 15-25x |
| **LTV:CAC (Doctor)** | 15-25x | 40-80x |

### LTV Calculation
- Average consultation value: $15
- Patient makes 6-10 consultations/year
- Patient stays 2-3 years
- Average patient LTV: $120-200
- Doctor earnings: $100/consultation × 50 consultations × 3 years = $15,000 LTV

## 6. Break-even Analysis

### Fixed Costs (Annual)
- Team (5-10 people): $200,000-300,000
- Infrastructure: $30,000-50,000
- Marketing: $50,000-100,000
- Legal/Compliance: $20,000-40,000
- **Total Fixed**: **$300,000-490,000**

### Break-even Calculation
- Average contribution per consultation: $1.25
- Break-even consultations: 300,000 ÷ 1.25 = **240,000/year** or **20,000/month**

### Timeline to Break-even
| Scenario | Break-even Point |
|----------|-----------------|
| Conservative | Month 30-36 |
| Base | Month 24-30 |
| Aggressive | Month 18-24 |
