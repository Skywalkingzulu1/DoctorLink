# DoctorLink - Doctor Onboarding Guide

Welcome to DoctorLink! This guide will help you get started on Africa's first gig economy telemedicine platform.

---

## What is DoctorLink?

DoctorLink is a telemedicine platform where doctors work as independent gig professionals. You set your own prices, choose your own schedule, and consult with patients via video call.

### Key Benefits
- **Flexible Schedule** - Work when you want
- **Set Your Own Prices** - Quick Chat, Video Call, Full Consultation
- **Earn More** - Keep 80% of consultation fees + 100% of tips
- **Gig Mode** - Go online/offline anytime

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Register as a Doctor

1. Visit the DoctorLink registration page
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Select "Doctor" as your role
4. Complete your profile (name, specialty, area)

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Required information:
- **Full Name** (as it appears on medical license)
- **Specialty** (e.g., General Practitioner, Pediatrics)
- **Practice Area** (city/region)
- **Consultation Fee** (base price)
- **HPCSA/MDCN/KMPDB Number** (for verification)
- **Bio** (short description for patients)

Optional:
- Profile photo
- Additional certifications
- Languages spoken

### Step 3: Set Your Service Prices

Configure your pricing for each service type:

| Service | Default | You Can Set |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Quick Chat (10 min) | 50 credits | 10-200 credits |
| Video Call (30 min) | 150 credits | 50-500 credits |
| Full Consultation (60 min) | 250 credits | 100-1000 credits |
| Prescription Review | 80 credits | 30-300 credits |
| Report Analysis | 120 credits | 50-500 credits |

You can also set **Peak Pricing** (up to 50% increase during busy hours).

### Step 4: Verify Your Credentials

For security and trust, all doctors must verify:
- Medical license number
- Identity verification
- (Optional) HPCSA/Medical Council verification for badge

## Using DoctorLink

### Going Online (Gig Mode)

1. Log in to your dashboard
2. Click "Go Online" button
3. You're visible to patients seeking consultations

### Taking Appointments

When a patient books:
1. You'll receive notification
2. Review patient info and reason
3. Accept or adjust timing
4. At appointment time, join video call

### During Consultations

- **Start Call** - Click to begin video consultation
- **Duration** - Timer shows consultation length
- **Notes** - Document important points
- **Prescribe** - Write e-prescription if needed

### Ending Consultations

1. Complete the call
2. Add notes if needed
3. Write prescription if medication needed
4. Mark consultation as complete
5. Patient can rate your service
6. Earn credits automatically (80% of fee)

## Managing Your Practice

### Dashboard Overview
- **Today's Schedule** - Upcoming appointments
- **Pending Requests** - New booking requests
- **Earnings** - Your income breakdown
- **Ratings** - Patient reviews

### Earnings & Payouts
- **80%** of consultation fee goes to you
- **100%** of any tips go to you
- **Platform** takes 20% fee

To collect earnings:
1. Go to Earnings section
2. Click "Collect Earnings"
3. Credits transfer to your wallet
4. Withdraw to bank account

### Setting Availability

**Quick Block** - Block specific hours today
**Schedule** - Set recurring weekly hours
**Vacation Mode** - Pause all bookings temporarily

## Best Practices

### For Great Consultations
1. **Be on time** - Join 2 minutes before scheduled
2. **Good lighting** - Face a light source
3. **Clear audio** - Use headphones if possible
4. **Professional background** - Clean, simple environment
5. **Patient focus** - Listen actively, explain clearly

### For Building Ratings
1. Greet patients warmly
2. Ask about their main concern
3. Explain diagnosis in simple terms
4. Provide clear next steps
5. Follow up if needed

### For Growing Your Practice
1. Complete your profile fully
2. Set competitive prices
3. Maintain good availability
4. Encourage patients to review you
5. Respond to requests promptly

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I work from home?**
A: Yes! All consultations are video calls from your location.

**Q: What if I need to cancel?**
A: You can decline appointment requests or use Vacation Mode.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Earnings go to your wallet. Collect anytime to bank.

**Q: Is there a minimum commitment?**
A: No - work as much or as little as you want.

**Q: What specialties can practice?**
A: Any licensed medical professional can join.

**Q: How does gig mode work?**
A: Toggle Go Online/Offline anytime. Only visible to patients when online.
