# DoctorLink - Patient User Guide

Welcome to DoctorLink! Get healthcare when you need it - from the comfort of your home.

---

## What is DoctorLink?

DoctorLink connects you with licensed doctors through video consultations. No waiting in lines, no traveling to clinics, just quality healthcare from your phone or computer.

### Why Use DoctorLink?
- **Quick** - Book and consult within minutes
- **Convenient** - Consult from anywhere
- **Affordable** - Transparent pricing, no hidden fees
- **Quality** - Verified, licensed doctors
- **Flexible** - Choose service type that fits your needs

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Account
1. Download app or visit website
2. Enter your email and password
3. Verify your phone number

### Step 2: Add Credits
- Go to Credits section
- Choose package or enter amount
- Pay via mobile money, card, or bank

### Step 3: Find a Doctor
- Browse doctors by specialty
- Check ratings and reviews
- View availability
- See pricing

### Step 4: Book Appointment
1. Select service type
2. Choose date/time
3. Describe your symptoms
4. Confirm booking

### Step 5: Consult
- Get notification when doctor joins
- Join video call
- Discuss your health
- Receive prescription if needed

## During Your Consultation

### Before Call
- Find quiet, private space
- Ensure good lighting
- Have any relevant documents ready
- Test your camera/microphone

### During Call
- Be clear about your main concern
- Ask questions if unsure
- Take notes on important points
- Don't hesitate to ask for clarification

### After Call
- Note any instructions
- Fill prescription at pharmacy
- Rate your experience
- Book follow-up if needed

## Tips for Best Experience

### For Better Consultations
1. **Be prepared** - Write down symptoms beforehand
2. **Be specific** - Describe exactly what you feel
3. **Be honest** - Share all relevant information
4. **Be patient** - Give doctor time to assess

### Getting Good Care
1. **Ask questions** - Don't leave unclear
2. **Take notes** - Write down instructions
3. **Follow up** - Book if symptoms persist
4. **Rate doctors** - Help others make decisions

## Troubleshooting

### Can't connect to video call?
- Check internet connection
- Try using different browser
- Ensure camera/mic permissions
- Contact support if persists

### Credits not deducted?
- Check balance sufficient
- Contact support for help

### Doctor didn't show?
- Wait 5 minutes
- Check appointment time
- Contact support

## Inconvenience Discounts

If you experience issues during a consultation (video problems, call drops, doctor late), you can report it to get a 10% discount on your next booking.

**How to apply:**
1. After call, click "Report Issue"
2. Select issue type
3. Discount automatically applies to next booking

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does a consultation take?**
A: Service duration varies - Quick Chat 10min, Video Call 30min, Full Consultation 60min.

**Q: Can I get a prescription?**
A: Yes, doctors can write e-prescriptions that you can fill at any pharmacy.

**Q: What if I need to cancel?**
A: Cancel anytime before appointment - credits are refunded.

**Q: Can I consult for my child?**
A: Yes, you can consult for dependents under 18.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use HIPAA-level encryption and protect your health data.

**Q: How do I get my medical records?**
A: Access your records in the app - you can share with other doctors.
